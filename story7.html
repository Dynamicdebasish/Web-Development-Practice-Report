The Lost Key

In a small village by the sea, there lived a young boy named Tom. One afternoon, while playing near the cliffs, he found an old, rusty key buried in the sand.

Curious, Tom rushed home to show his grandmother. She gasped, recognizing the key immediately.

"This is the key to the forgotten lighthouse," she said. "No one has opened its doors in over a hundred years."

With excitement in his heart, Tom knew what he had to do. The next morning, he set out toward the lighthouse, determined to unlock its secrets.
