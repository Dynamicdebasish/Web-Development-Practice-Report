The Mysterious Door

It was a cold and rainy night when Emily discovered the door. Hidden in the forest behind her house, the door seemed out of place, standing upright with no walls around it.

Curiosity overwhelmed her, and despite her better judgment, she stepped closer. There was an ancient symbol carved into the wood, glowing faintly.

Without thinking, Emily reached for the handle. The moment her fingers touched it, the ground trembled and the door swung open.

What Lies Beyond

Beyond the door was not the forest, but a world she had never seen before. The sky was a brilliant shade of purple, and floating islands drifted lazily in the distance. Strange creatures roamed freely, their eyes glowing with the same light as the symbol.

"Where am I?" she whispered, stepping through the doorway.

The door slammed shut behind her. Emily spun around, but it had vanished. She was trapped in this new world.

And her adventure was only just beginning.
